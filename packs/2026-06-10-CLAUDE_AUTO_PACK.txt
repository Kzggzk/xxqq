LTL Freight Set for Potential Rebound in 2026, but Uncertainty Looms 
North American less-than-truckload freight market analysis finding early indicators of volume firming and modest rate improvement, but with significant uncertainty from tariff policy changes, potential rail labor disruption, and inconsistent demand recovery across manufacturing and retail sectors. The report projects a modest LTL recovery contingent on macroeconomic stabilization.
